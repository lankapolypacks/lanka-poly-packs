Courier Bags for Instagram Businesses in Sri Lanka

How Instagram sellers can choose colours, sizes and packaging combinations for a better customer experience.

Sri Lankan online sellers often need packaging that is simple, affordable and reliable. The best choice depends on the product, customer expectation and delivery method. Clothing sellers usually need courier bags or frosted ziplock bags, while small delicate products may need bubble mailers for extra protection.

For Instagram sellers, TikTok sellers and home businesses, packaging is part of the customer experience. A neat courier bag, clean inner packing and fast WhatsApp ordering can make repeat customers feel more confident. For Daraz sellers and daily ecommerce orders, consistent size selection and stock availability are also important.

How to choose packaging

Start with the product size and weight. Use smaller courier bags for single lightweight items, medium courier bags for common clothing orders and larger bags for bulkier parcels. Add frosted ziplock bags when presentation matters. Use bubble mailers when the item needs padded protection.
